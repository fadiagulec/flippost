# FlipIt — 6-Day Launch Push

Cohesive 6-day arc to drive the FlipIt launch. Each day = **1 Reel + 1 Carousel + 1 Static post**, posted in that order across the day.

**Product anchors** (locked across all copy):
- Tagline: *See It. Flip It. Post It. Go Viral.*
- Hero promise: paste any viral URL → FlipIt rewrites it as a fresh script with a new hook in 30 seconds.
- Pricing: **$37 lifetime, no subs**.
- 4-in-1: script flipper, hook generator, caption + hashtag pack, watermark-free downloader.
- DM funnel: comment trigger word → auto-DM Stripe link.
- Voice: Fadia Podcast Voice 3 / Gaia avatar (HeyGen).

**Daily rhythm:**
- 9:00 AM ET — Static post drops (warm-up)
- 11:00 AM ET — Reel drops (peak reach)
- 6:00 PM ET — Carousel drops (evening save-bait)
- All day — Stories reposting both, with link sticker

**Arc:**
| Day | Theme | Trigger | Emotional beat |
|-----|-------|---------|----------------|
| 1 | LAUNCH — "It's live" | FLIPIT | Excitement, novelty |
| 2 | PROBLEM — why creators stall | BLANK | Recognition, pain |

| 6 | CLOSE — last call before price moves | LAST | Urgency, FOMO |
